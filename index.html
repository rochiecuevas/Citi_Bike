Citi Bike Usage in New York (2018)

Citi Bikes is a bike rental program that allows bikers to undock a bike, ride it for as long as their passes are valid, then redock the bike at any Citi Bike station. Single passes are USD 3.00/trip; with each trip lasting up to 30 minutes.

This page attempts to get some insights into how people use Citi Bikes: Who uses Citi Bikes? Where do people go when they use Citi Bikes? What does Citi Bike station traffic flow look like on any given day in 2018?
